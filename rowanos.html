OUR STORY & VISION

Welcome to Our Family-Friendly Restaurant & Pizzeria

Proudly serving the communities of Croydon, Seville, and Chirnside Park. We're dedicated to creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where families and friends can gather, share delicious meals, and make lasting memories.

With a menu that caters to all ages and tastes, our pizzas and dishes are a result of years of honing our craft and perfecting our recipes. Whether you're a seasoned pizza connoisseur or just looking for a wholesome meal with loved ones, Rowanos is sure to satisfy.
