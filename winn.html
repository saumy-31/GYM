DISCOVER BRISBANE'S HIDDEN WONDERLAND

A Vibrant Creative Hive Behind Shannon's Building

Hidden behind the historic Shannon's building—which houses the iconic Zoo live music venue—this small laneway is a hive of activity for Brisbane's creative types and those seeking something different.

"Likened to 'falling into a rabbit hole' and slipping into a 'wonderland' of shops and people..."

— Winn Lane Precinct Guide

Winn Lane is home to a vibrant, creative community of local and independent artisans, fashion designers, hair stylists, and foodies serving indulgent café treats and classic burgers. Whether it is vintage, cult, or contemporary, there are many treasures to be found here.
