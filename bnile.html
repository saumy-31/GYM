Satisfying the Discerning Palate of Pune Across Generational Eras

Established in 1970 along Bund Garden Road, Blue Nile Restaurant stands proud as one of Pune's most iconic and long-standing dining landmarks. Renowned universally for containing the absolute best biryani in the city, our heritage kitchen is designed around rich spices, timeless secret marinations, and pristine ingredients.

We blend pure Mughlai density alongside flawless North Indian tandoor dynamics. Every platter represents a deep history of culinary excellence combined with swift, high-standard service crafted perfectly for large, energetic family dining clusters.
