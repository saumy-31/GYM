Our History

A Beloved Staple in the Local Community

Rico's Restaurant has been proudly serving the community for over 7 years. What started as a modest, passionate family-owned dream has beautifully blossomed into a cherished local establishment.

We design an immersive atmosphere featuring a warm and welcoming ambiance that is absolutely perfect for any occasion. Every recipe celebrates a blend of regional profiles showcasing true culinary heritage, delivered by a friendly staff dedicated to making you feel right at home.
