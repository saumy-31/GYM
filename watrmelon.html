About Our Shop

A Whimsical Haven in the Heart of Hampden

Located at 3555 Chestnut Avenue in Baltimore, MD, In Watermelon Sugar is more than just a boutique—it is a celebration of creativity, joy, and thoughtful gifting. Inspired by poetry and artistic wonder, our shop brings together an eclectic mix of home accessories, paper goods, jewelry, candles, and seasonal delights.

Whether you are hunting for the perfect birthday present, a housewarming token, or simply treating yourself to something special, our welcoming atmosphere and friendly staff are always here to make your visit unforgettable.
