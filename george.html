OUR STORIES & LINEAGE

Serving Pune's Most Celebrated Gastronomic Legacies For Generations

Since opening our heavy wooden doors in 1936, George Restaurant has cemented itself as an unshakeable culinary sanctuary in the heart of East Street, Camp, Pune. Our kitchen stands historic as the authentic home of meticulously layered Biryanis and masterfully flame-grilled artisan Kababs.

We balance deep North Indian structural complexity alongside highly traditional Persian delicacies. Every plate represents an ancestral collection of aromatics, precise temperature conditioning, and timeless hospitality designed to bring unmatched delight directly to your dynamic family gatherings.

Persian Specialities Taste heritage classics including our aromatic Chello Mutton minced grills and tender Jujeh Kabab cubes.
