Our Philosophy

A tribute to the beauty of ingredients.

At Evra, we treasure the natural world by transforming the purity of earth and sea into a warm, welcoming farm-to-table experience. Each dish is thoughtfully prepared to celebrate the deep connection between nature and the table.

Guided by the seasons, we work closely with Australian farmers, including the renowned CopperTree Farms, and source wild-caught fish from the pristine waters of the Great Barrier Reef.

We also proudly harvest from our zero-km rooftop farm, bringing the country's freshest flavours to life in a decadent atmosphere of urbane sophistication, immersed in lush greenery.

Zero-Km Harvest

We proudly harvest herbs and micro-greens directly from our zero-km rooftop farm, bringing unparalleled freshness to your plate.

Earth & Sea

Partnering with CopperTree Farms and sourcing wild-caught fish from the pristine waters of the Great Barrier Reef.

Curated Cellar

A carefully curated wine list completes the experience, celebrated in an atmosphere of urbane sophistication.
