CONTINUING THE BIJOU LEGACY AT THE CHURCH

Bijou Restaurant is transitioning to one of Stratford's most iconic architectural landmarks. Moving from 34 Brunswick to the historic church property at 70 Brunswick Street, the property will launch under a refreshed vision: Bijou at the Church.

The new identity intentionally honors two deeply respected names within Stratford's rich hospitality history: the celebrated "Bijou Restaurant", which has served guests beautifully for nearly three decades, and the legendary "Church Restaurant", recognized globally as one of the city's most memorable destinations.
