Underbar is a chef-led restaurant in Ballarat, cooking exclusively on Friday and Saturday nights. This limitation ensures an uncompromising dedication to quality and preparation.

Our philosophy is rooted in focused, precise cooking built around exceptional produce. The seasonal menu is a direct reflection of the region's finest ingredients sourced directly from local growers and foragers at their absolute peak.
