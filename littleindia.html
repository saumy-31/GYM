Welcoming Families Through Our Doors Since January 2, 1995

We first opened our doors over two decades ago, and we haven't looked back since. Little India introduced truly authentic and highly traditional Indian food to the Toronto area, growing into what many say is the absolute gold-standard destination for Dine-In, Delivery, Pickup, and event Catering.

Our beloved customers deeply appreciate that we remain family owned and operated, continuously bringing our favorite timeless recipes directly to your plate. Beyond the kitchen, we are passionately involved in supporting local non-profit organizations and charities, giving back entirely to the community that has sustained us for so many years.
