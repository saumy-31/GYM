About Coco Kanela

Connecting Women with Nature's Healing Properties.

Cocokanela Design is an Australian-based boho lifestyle brand. We are founded with the primary objective of helping women connect with nature and the earth's healing properties through the utilisation of gemstones and crystal healing.

Traditional Mexican artisan skills and culture are expressed through the colours and style of our jewellery. We integrate these techniques with contemporary design to create unique, marketable products crafted with love.
