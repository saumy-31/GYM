Our Story

Seclusion and Personal Space To Truly Relax

Located beautifully in Downham Market, Norfolk, The Old Gym was established to provide an alternative to crowded public leisure complexes. We pride ourselves on offering a completely private, serene environment where security, peace, and premium relaxation merge effortlessly.

Whether you are setting up a family fun afternoon, celebrating an intimate gathering, or seeking physical recovery in our optimally balanced heated pool and deep heat sauna, we give you the entire venue to yourself.
