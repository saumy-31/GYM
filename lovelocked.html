Our Story

Welcome to Love Locked

For over 8 years, we've curated and crafted the finest pieces of jewelry in the heart of Jersey City. Our boutique specializes in unique, one-of-a-kind treasures that are as mindful of the earth as they are beautiful.

We partner with small independent businesses and over 160 artisans from around the world. Every stone, every object, and every metal that sits in our shop is strictly vetted for ethical sourcing.

"I've taken great care to provide an intimate experience at Love Locked where people can enter and feel delighted by all the treasures within. My team provides one-on-one assistance to ensure they find the perfect piece."
— Kerry Lynn, Founder

Frequently Asked Questions

We start with a consultation to understand your vision, budget, and timeline. From there, we begin a "treasure hunt" for the perfect ethically-sourced stones. Once stones are selected, we collaborate on sketches and CAD renderings before finalizing the piece in our workshop.

We meticulously vet every vendor, miner, and artisan we partner with. We prioritize recycled metals and trace our stones directly to mines that adhere to strict fair-trade and environmentally conscious mining practices, like our trusted Montana Sapphire sources.

To provide the highest level of intimate, one-on-one service—especially for complex bespoke and wedding jewelry consultations—we are currently operating via private appointments. This ensures our team can dedicate uninterrupted time to finding or crafting your perfect piece.
