Conceived in 1994

A Story Still Being Written...

1000 Oaks is a way of life for many of its characters. Having grown up with generations of patrons, we have proudly served them their first drink, witnessed their first intoxication, laughed together, and offered a shoulder to cry on occasionally.

We've played the soundtracks of your milestones, watched friendships bloom into marriage, and welcomed children into parenthood. Today, as we invite the next generation into our tribe, we remain committed to taking our music as seriously as our food and drink.
